10-week internship conducted during my first year of engineering school, within the **Product Team** at Cardiologs — an AI-powered ECG and Holter diagnostic platform (acquired by Philips in 2021).

**Mission**: Improve the onboarding experience for cardiologist users and increase accessibility of help resources.

- Weekly meetings with a cardiologist and the sales team to collect user needs and pain points
- Weekly meetings with the Product team to follow ongoing platform development
- Weekly meetings with the Design team to prototype and iterate on solutions
- The integrated solution was shipped into the Cardiologs platform in **October 2021**
